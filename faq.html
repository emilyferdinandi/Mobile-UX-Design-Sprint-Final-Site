Who Should Attend

We recommend 3-5 attendees, including: 1 marketing decision maker on site content, 1-3 designers or product managers to prototype changes, and 1 developer to confirm technical feasibility of changes

Project Scope

This Sprint Workshop will focus on improving the conversion flow of your mobile website. Mobile app projects or projects focused on a logged in user experience are not a good fit. You will want to choose a project that is a high priority internally and can be tested/implemented.

In the past, clients have worked on consolidating a check out process, implementing Google APIs, and removing friction points from forms
