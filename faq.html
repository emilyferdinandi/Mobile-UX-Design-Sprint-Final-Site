Who Should Attend

We recommend 3-5 attendees, including: 1 marketing decision maker on site content, 1-3 designers or product managers to prototype changes, and 1 developer to confirm technical feasibility of changes

Project Scope

This Sprint Workshop will focus on improving the conversion flow of your mobile website (mobile app projects will not be allowed). You will want to choose a project that is a high priority internally and can be tested/implimented.

Many clients choose to work on optimizing an application form or checkout process, while some work on the homepage or content pages. Either way, we suggest focusing on either new users or upsells, not a logged in user experience.
