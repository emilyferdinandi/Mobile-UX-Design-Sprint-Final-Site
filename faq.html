Who Should Attend

You can bring between 2-5 people to the sprint (3-4 is ideal). You will will want to have representation from different teams, with each sprint member contributing their unique skill set. It is essential to have someone with decision making power over the mobile site. In addition we often see representation from web design, UX design, front end web developement, product management, and marketing.

Project Scope

The focus of this sprint is improving your conversion flow for your mobile website (mobile app projects will not be allowed). Many clients choose to work on optimizing an application form or checkout process, while some work on the homepage or content pages. Either way, we suggest focusing on either new users or upsells, not a logged in user experience.
